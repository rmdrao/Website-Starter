## About the Role

As a UX Designer at Zenith, you'll be responsible for creating user-centered designs that balance business goals with user needs. You'll work on various digital products, from concept to implementation, ensuring a seamless and delightful user experience.

## What You'll Do

- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Collaborate with developers to ensure accurate implementation of designs
- Contribute to and maintain our design system
- Stay current with UX trends and best practices
- Present design concepts to stakeholders and incorporate feedback

## Why Zenith?

At Zenith, we believe that great design is at the heart of exceptional products. We foster a creative environment where designers have the freedom to innovate and the support to grow professionally. Join our team and help shape the future of digital experiences that make a difference.

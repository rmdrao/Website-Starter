## About the Role

As a Product Designer at Zenith, you'll be responsible for creating intuitive, engaging user experiences that meet both user needs and business goals. You'll collaborate with product managers, engineers, and other designers to bring ideas from concept to reality.

## What You'll Do

- Design user-centered interfaces for web and mobile applications
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
- Develop and maintain our design system and component library
- Collaborate with engineers to ensure accurate implementation of designs
- Participate in design critiques and provide constructive feedback

## Why Zenith?

- Competitive salary and comprehensive benefits package
- Flexible work arrangements with remote options
- Professional development budget for courses and conferences
- Collaborative, creative work environment with talented peers
- Opportunity to shape products used by millions of people
- Regular team events and company-wide activities

## Requirements

- Bachelor's degree in Design, HCI, or equivalent practical experience
- 3+ years of professional product design experience
- Strong understanding of user-centered design principles
- Experience working in cross-functional product teams
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Ability to give and receive constructive feedback
